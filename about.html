What Is This

人肉搜索 (rénròu sōusuǒ) translates literally as "human flesh search engine." It originated in early Chinese internet culture. When someone wronged the collective and the platforms wouldn't act, the crowd did. They found the person. They mapped the person. They made the person legible.

This is the same thing, built for a different problem.

Every person who operates in public is running influence patterns. Most of them don't know they're doing it. Some of them know exactly what they're doing. FLESHENGINE makes the patterns visible.

This is not a doxxing tool. We don't collect addresses, phone numbers, or private data. We collect patterns. How someone frames a narrative. How they manufacture urgency. How they exploit trust. The things that are technically public but functionally invisible until someone maps them.

How It Works

A target is submitted. Public data is extracted from whatever platforms they operate on. The scanner runs twelve vulnerability vectors against their output. Human observers contribute fragments: behavioral patterns, relationship dynamics, narrative shifts they've noticed. The fragments accumulate. The profile sharpens.

The result is a composite intelligence brief. Not who someone is. Who someone does.

The Twelve Vectors

Every influence operation, whether deliberate or instinctive, runs on a combination of these:

FE-001Narrative FramingControlling what the story is about before anyone asks
FE-002Repetition SaturationMaking something true by making it unavoidable
FE-003Audience ConditioningTraining a group to receive messages in a specific way
FE-004Loyalty ExploitationWeaponizing trust, belonging, and obligation
FE-005Identity ManipulationTargeting who someone believes they are
FE-006Manufactured ConsensusMaking a minority position feel like the default
FE-007Viral PropagationTurning people into vectors for content they didn't create
FE-008Urgency FabricationCompressing decision time to prevent reflection
FE-009Attention RedirectionControlling what you look at by controlling what you don't
FE-010Reality DistortionMaking the constructed feel inevitable
FE-011Cognitive OverloadFlooding channels until critical thinking drowns
FE-012Existential Threat FramingEscalating stakes until compliance feels like survival

Who Built This

FLESHENGINE is an open-source project. The vulnerability framework is adapted from research by the Seithar Group.

The source code is on GitHub. You can run your own instance.

Ethics

The people who need to be mapped are the people who will object to being mapped. This is by design. Influence operators depend on illegibility. Legibility is the countermeasure.

FLESHENGINE profiles public behavior. If you don't want to be profiled, stop running influence operations in public.

If that sounds harsh, consider what happens when nobody maps these patterns at all.
